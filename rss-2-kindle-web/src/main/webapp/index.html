About

RSS-2-KINDLE is a free of charge and absolutely noncommercial service that allows you to redirect your RSS-subscriptions to your favorite kindle device. All you need is just sign up and create a subscriber for your kindle

The service is provided "AS IS" without warranty of any kind. It is an open source project and code is available on github

Why

Do you love your little kindle? Do you you feel boring during commute but always forget to upload new articles into your kindle? Would you enjoy reading your favourite feeds in kindle? Do you have several kindle devices? This service for you: Unlimited number of subscribers You can create a separated subscriber for each of your kindle devices. Manage your subscriptions for each kindle You can manage list of subscriptions for each kindle device independently Pretty view Each channel comes to kindle as a book Flexibility You can set up any email for subscriber, not only kindle email.

How it works

Create a subscriber. Usually it corresponds to email of your kindle Create a list of subscriptions for the subscriber. It is just list of URLs of RSS-feeds Add email of this service into access list in your amazon account And vualya - every 24 hours the service will be checking updates in your feeds, convert them into mobi format and send them into subscriber's email
